Why I Am Currently Dating A 27-Year-Old Man

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, May 6, 2024

Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing Sunday has given reasons why she is romantically involved with someone who is in his late twenties.

The controversial actress made this known during a recent interview with the women of ‘Your View’ TV program where she noted that she is very young at heart, hence her preference for a younger man.

She added that the decision also came after the very bitter and public end of her relationship with her ex-boyfriend, Opeyemi Falegan.

“I am actually very young at heart, in my inside. He is a younger man. He’s 27, I’m 33. He makes me feel like a baby, hello. We are friends for now. We are not trying to define what we have now, we are just going with the flow because right now he makes me happy. Like my vibe matches his vibe and we are cool like that”.

When asked if the level of maturity won’t affect the relationship, she said, ”Right now, I just want to be happy. So I am actually going with the flow. Cuz a lot of people be like “ah ah, when did you break up that you are dating again” hello, I was raised with love.”

“February 14th, I’m Valentina. I have so much love in me. I can give out so much love. So why not? When I see something that I like, that matches my vibe. So I don’t know, I’m just going with the flow. I’m not even concerned about breakfast or no breakfast because if it happens, we move,” the actress said.
