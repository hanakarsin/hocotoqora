Nicole Frye - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, June 7, 2024

Social star who rocketed to fame after she got engaged to YouTube star and BMX rider Adam LZ. Through him, she inherited a following of more than 90,000 Instagram fans.

Before Fame

One of her early appearances on Adam's channel was in June 2014 in the video "Webisode 19: Say Something, I'm giving up on BMX."

Trivia

She has appeared in videos on Adam's channel such as "Nicole's Miata: Exhaust, Speedo Fix, and Steering Wheel!" and "15 MINUTE COILOVERS."

Family Life

She and Adam LZ married in 2016; but would divorce in 2018.

Associated With

Her fiancee is the popular sports YouTuber like Tanner Fox.
