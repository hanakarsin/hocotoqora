Prophet Reveals Next Governor Of Lagos

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

With the forthcoming 2015 general elections fast approaching, there has been many predictions, revelations by some men of God on the outcome of the elections.

Prophet Owatunde Samuel, a.k.a. Marshal of CCC Trinity Tabernacle Parish, Lagos, in a recent interview with PM News revealed who the next President of Nigeria, Governor of Lagos State will be and the fate of Boko Haram.

Speaking on who the next Governor of Lagos will be, Prophet Samuel said: "The next governor of Lagos is going to be a Christian; there is no doubt about that. I will not mention his name because of ministerial ethics. This coming 2015, APC will have upper hand more than PDP despite the fact that PDP is incumbent government."

On who will become the President of Nigeria come 2015 he said: "President Goodluck Jonathan will return, there is no doubt about it."

"Jonathan is destined to be the president again. But we need to pray concerning that because there will be more bloodshed. People who are contesting should have the fear of God and be committed to genuinely serve the people."

Prophet Samuel who also spoke on the issue of Boko Haram in the country said:

"God is going to disappoint many political godfathers. Towards the end of this year there will be no Boko Haram again in Nigeria.

"Go and write it down. I will not say more than I have said already."

Meanwhile Prophet Samuel said God has revealed several things about President Jonathan that has come to pass. Speaking of prophecies coming to pass he said: " In July, I predicted that Governor Rauf Aregbesola will return".

Prophet Owatunde Samuel joined Celestial Church at the age of 18, trained as a fashion designer and practiced from 1986 to 1990.

Prophet Samuel who started his ministry 30 years ago is set to celebrate his 50th birthday as well as his 25th wedding anniversary to his wife, Christianah Owatunde.

Meanwhile Rev. Moses Iloh, a respected cleric, General Overseer of Soul Winning Chapel, Ebute-Metta and President, Eclectic Network, has warned President Goodluck Jonathan not to run for a second tenure in the forthcoming 2015 elections.
