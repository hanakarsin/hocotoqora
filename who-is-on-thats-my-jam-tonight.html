Who Is on Thats My Jam Tonight? Meet the Four New Celebrities

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, July 26, 2024

Who is on 'That's My Jam' tonight? Each episode features a new group of celebrities competing on the show. Let's learn about them now.

Season 2 of That’s My Jam debuted on March 7, and the line-up of A-list talent has not disappointed.

The Jimmy Fallon–hosted musical game show’s premiere started strong with guest stars Kelsea Ballerini, Julia Michaels, Jason Derulo, and Nicole Scherzinger — and the guests have continued to impress with each ep.

Which four celebrities will appear on the April 24 episode of That’s My Jam? We have the details!

Who is on 'That's My Jam' tonight, April 24, 2023?

The musical game show is bringing in the heat for tonight’s episode, airing April 24 at 10 p.m. EST on NBC. The new faces feature a mix of actors, comedians, and rappers.

Check 'em out below!

Kenan Thompson will appear on 'That's My Jam.'

Kenan Thompson is a comedy icon who holds the title of the longest-running cast member on Saturday Night Live. Over the years, Kenan has also appeared in a slew of TV series, comedy sketch shows, and films that include Nickelodeon's All That, Barbershop 2: Back in Business, Good Burger, and more.

Big Boi will make an appearance on 'That's My Jam.'

Next up, we have Big Boi. The rap icon and one-half of the duo Outkast will also be making an appearance on That's My Jam. Avid hip-hop fans admire Big Boi for his unique skills as an MC and for his influence on the rap world today.

Kesha will dazzle viewers on 'That's My Jam.'

Singer and songwriter Kesha will also be in the building for the April 24 episode of That's My Jam. The hitmaker has penned multiple No. 1 songs including "Timber," "Tik Tok," and "We R Who We R." It'll be interesting to watch how she performs on the show.

Renée Elise Goldsberry rounds out the list of guest stars on 'That's My Jam.'

Last but certainly not least, we have Renée Goldsberry. The beauty is an actress and singer who portrayed Angelica Schuyler in the Broadway musical Hamilton. Renee later won the 2016 Tony Award for Best Featured Actress in a Musical for her portrayal of Angelica.

Who appeared on 'That's My Jam' previously?

During the April 17 episode of That's My Jam, audiences were entertained by singer Adam Lambert, multi-hyphenates Chloe and Halle Bailey, and actor Simu Liu.

Catch new episodes of That’s My Jam, Mondays at 10 p.m. EST on NBC.
