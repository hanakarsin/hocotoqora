Messi vs Ronaldo vs Neymar: who is the best player?

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, April 19, 2024

Who is the best player of all times? Fans keep discussing the rivalry between three most popular football superstars! Messi vs Ronaldo vs Neymar – who is the best of them all? Let's take a detailed look at the rivalry between these three players.

Rivalry Between Footballers

Messi vs Ronaldo, Neymar vs Messi and Ronaldo vs Neymar There's obviously a rival and healthy competiton between these amazing players, but we do not believe that they actually hate each other.

Messi vs Ronaldo

Messi and Ronaldo both won FIFA Ballon d`Or. Messi won this award five times, and the same can be said about Ronaldo. They managed to break the 50 goal barrier in a season. Moreover, they scored over 600 goals for their international career.

Some commentators even try to discuss the different physiques of two players. Moreover, these two players have the different style of football. Ronaldo, for instance, is one of the persons who prefer to show off. It can be even viewed as a theatrical show.

The rivalry continues at the club levels. Ronaldo represents Real Madrid and Messi plays for Barcelona. These two players face each other at least once in every season.

It's interesting that both players are rivals at Airline Levels – Qatar Airways and Emirates Airlines. They are also two highest paid footballers in the world. Ronaldo earns about 88 million dollars per year. At the same time, Messi gets about 81.4 million dollars per year.

Compare to these two experienced players. Neymar is just a starter in the football. Nevertheless, he shows interesting skills which can help him to become the best football player in the future. It seems only the beginning of his path to greater fame!

Neymar Football Background

Neymar was born on February 5, 1992. He is currently 26 years old. his full name is Neymar da Silva Santos Junior. He is a Brazilian football player who is also a member of Paris Saint-Germain FC. He is also a member of Brazilian National Team.

Neymar made his professional debut when he was only 17. Neymar is considered to the fourth highest goal scorer for the Brazilian National Team. He managed to captain the Brazilian National Team through the Olympic Games in 2016. Therefore, this team won their first gold medal.

Neymar is also well known for his dribbling abilities. Some football commentators may compare this young man with the football superstar – Pele.

Lionel Messi Background

Messi was born on June 24, 1987. His place of birth is a small town Rosario in Santa Fe Province. He was spotted at the very early age. At 13, he progressed to the Barcelona's youth Academy. When he only started his football career, he managed to get a tag – young Maradona. Messi is a holder of various football records.

Messi is called the all-time goal scorer. He managed to score seven UEFA Champions League hat-tricks. Messi has scored over five hundred goals in his international career. Messi is the gold medalist of Olympic Games 2008.

Cristiano Ronaldo Background

Cristiano Ronaldo is the oldest amongst Messi and Neymar. Ronaldo was born in Funchal of Madeira. His senior debut was in 2002. Ronaldo was one of the main players of Manchester United for almost six years. He helped Manchester to win three consecutive Premier League Titles. When he left United, he managed to score 118 goals.

He was considered the most expensive football player in the world when he moved to Madrid. The price for his transfer was about ninety-four million euro. When he completed the eight season as a player of Real Madrid – he managed to score more than 400 goals. He still holds the record for the most goals in the Single Champions League Season.

What about the rivalry between Messi and Ronaldo? These two partners have a lot of time to fight each other on the football field. It's obvious that fans will continue to compare and define who is better. There will be various criteria to decide if Messi is better or if Ronaldo is better.
