About the Stockdale family

The Stockdale family includes Jacob Stockdale and the late Kathryn Barbara Stockdale, the parents of Calvin Stockdale, Charles Stockdale, and the late James William Stockdale. Jacob is the family’s fiddler, known for winning the Ohio States Grand Fiddle Championship. The family lived on their family farm in Ohio.

Who was Kathryn Stockdale?

Kathy Stockdale was described as a loving mother, grandmother, daughter, sister, aunt, friend, and mentor. She was a staunch Christian known for disciplining and instilling the best values in her sons. Kathryn had done everything right while raising her sons, including limiting their access to obvious pleasures such as video games, dating, and TV.

Kathryn had also trained her children on house chores as she had an elaborate system that saw the boys get rewarded by tokens, and once one had earned enough tokens, one would turn that in for recreational privileges. For instance, twenty tokens would win one a ticket to listen to a family-friendly approved show.

The Stockdale family band

What happened to the Stockdale family?

The Stockdale family was doing well until a tragic turn of events happened. Jacob Stockdale of the Stockdale family Band pleaded guilty to murdering his brother James and mother in 2017. Jacob confessed to shooting both his mother and brother in the head with a 20 gauge shotgun before turning the gun on himself.

He murdered them at their home in Stark County, Ohio. Jacob sustained a gunshot wound that put him in a critical medical condition at the MetroHealth Medical Center. His older brothers had long moved out of the Stork County home when this incident happened. Their Jacob Stockdale’s father was also not at home when all this was happening.
