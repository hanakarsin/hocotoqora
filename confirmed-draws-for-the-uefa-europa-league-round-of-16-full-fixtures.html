Confirmed Draws For The UEFA Europa League Round Of 16 (Full Fixtures)

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, August 17, 2024

The European football governing body, UEFA has released the full list of fixtures for the Europa League round of 16 games.

This follows the draw which was streamed live on Friday on the UEFA website.

Below are the full fixtures.

Rangers vs Crvena Zvezda

Braga vs Monaco

Atalanta vs Leverkusen

Porto vs Lyon

Barcelona vs Galatasaray

Sevilla vs West Ham

Betis vs Frankfurt

Leipzig vs Spartak Moskva

Meanwhile, European football governing body, UEFA on Friday ruled out Russia’s St. Petersburg stadium as the venue for this season’s Champions League final.

Naija News understands that the decision was a form of punishment and expression of disappointment over Russia’s invasion of Ukraine in the last 48hours.

After an extraordinary meeting on Friday morning to discuss the geopolitical crisis, UEFA announced that the Champions League final will now be played in Paris, the capital city of France.
