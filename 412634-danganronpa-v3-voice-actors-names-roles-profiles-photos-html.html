Danganronpa v3 voice actors: names, roles, profiles, and photos

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, June 14, 2024

Danganronpa V3 is a visual novel mystery game developed and released in Japan in 2017 for PlayStation 4 and PlayStation Vita. It is also available in America. Players enjoy the entire gaming experience by interacting with the images and text on the screen. The Danganronpa V3 voice actors represent various characters who carry the story's plot from start to finish.

Danganronpa V3 voice actors are crucial in making the visual game an unforgettable experience because they are primarily heard, not seen. Each character has a Japanese and an English voice edition. The players choose to play a character of their choice like they do in video games.

Danganronpa V3 voice actors

The visual novel mystery game boasts a wide range of talents voicing the characters on Danganronpa v3 in English. Each character in the series has a unique role that develops the story as the player goes along. Below are the current Danganronpa v3 English voice actors:

1. Erika Harlacher

Erika Harlacher is an American voice actress born on August 29, 1990, in Ventura County, California. She is currently based in Los Angeles and is married to Luke Stone. The actress has done voice work for English dubbed anime shows and video games.

Some of her work includes Elizabeth Liones in The Seven Deadly Sins, Asseylum Vers Allusia in Aldnoah. In Danganropa, Erika's voice role is the English dub voice of Kaede Akamatsu. Sayaka Kanda plays the Japanese voice.

2. Johnny Yong Bosch

Johnny Yong Bosch was born on January 6, 1976, in Kansas City, Missouri, USA, as John Jay Bosch. He holds mixed ethnicity of American and Korean. Johnny is a voice actor, musician, and martial artist.

The 45-year-old has been married to Amy Bosch since March 21, 2003. They have two children. He is known for his work on Ark Exitus (2020), Akira (1988), and Bleach (2004). Johnny is also famous for voicing older teenage boys in anime and video games. On Danganronpa, Johnny is Rantaro Amami voice actor. Hikaru Midorikawa does the Japanese voice.

3. Wendee Lee

Wendee Lee Spindler is an American voice actress born on 20 February 1960 in Los Angeles, California. She is also referred to as Elyse Floyd or Wendee Swan. The 61-year-old is also a singer, writer, and director.

As part of the Danganronpa v3 English cast, Wendee voices Miu Iruma. Haruka Ishida is the Japanese voice. The actress has also featured in Cowboy Bebop: The Movie (2001).

4. Derek Stephen Prince

Derek Stephen was born on 5 February 1969 in Inglewood, California, United States. The American voice actor is married to Amy Hollander, and they have three children. He also goes by the names David Umansky, Steven Prince, or Steve Prince.

Derek has featured in anime, animations, video games, and audiobooks. In Danganropa, he plays the English voice of Kokichi Oma. Hiro Shimono is the Japanese voice. Derek's work also includes the voice of Elgar in Power Rangers Turbo and Power Rangers in Space.

5. Lucien Dodge

Lucien Dodge was born in Ithaca, New York, USA, on June 24, 1984. He is known for his work in anime, animation, and video games. His work includes Maron and Chilli on Pokemon.

Lucien Dodge was born on June 24, 1984, in Ithaca, New York, USA as Lucien P. Dodge. He is known for his work on Logan (2017), Fate/Zero (2011), and MGS: Philanthropy (2009). Lucien is on the Danganronpa v3 English dub cast playing the role of Keebo. Tetsuya Kakihara is the Japanese voice.

6. Kaiji Tang

Kaiji is a Chinese voice actor based in Los Angeles. He was born in January 1984 in Shanghai, China. The actor has featured in over 50 games and more than 15 movies, including the 2020 TV show, Beastars.

Kaiji is the English dub voice of Gonta Gokuhara in Danganronpa v3. Shunsuke Takeuchi plays the Japanese voice.

7. Grant George

Grant George is an American voice artist born in May 1971. He is married to Jessica Gee, also a successful voice artist, and they have two children. The actor is famous for voicing animes and video games. He is also an on-camera actor.

In the Danganronpa visual novel, Grant is Shuichi Saihara voice actor. He has also featured in The Academy of Music (2020) and Elena of Avalor (2017). One of his most prominent roles is the voice of AntMan in Avengers Assemble (2013).

8. Dorothy Fahn

Dorothy is an American voice actress formerly known as Dorothy Melendrez. She was born in Los Angeles, California, in March 1962 and is married to Tom Fahn.

The actress has provided her voice in animes like Monica Rial, playing Sayaka's English voice, and Kara Edwards, where she does Chihiro and Alter Ego's English voice. In Danganronpa, Dorothy is the English dub voice of Tsumugi Shirogane, while Mikako Komatsu is the Japanese voice.

9. Todd Haberkorn

Todd is an American voice actor and director born on 16th August 1982 in Arlington, Texas. He is also referred to as Todd Stone. The actor was married to Bonnie Vanwinkle from 2008 to 2010 and is a father of one.

Haberkorn is the English dub voice of Korekiyo Shinguji, while Kenichi Suzumura plays the Japanese voice. He has been in the voice acting business since 2006 and has worked on productions like Kingsglaive: Final Fantasy XV (2016) and A Whisker Away (2020).

10. Julie Ann Taylor

Julie Ann Taylor, born Julie Ann Pickering, is an American voice-over artist born on January 29, 1966, in Fort Irwin, California. Julie is married to Cory Taylor, and they have one child.

The voice artist doubles as a writer. Her work includes Akira (1988), Last Exile (2003), and Bleach (2004). In Danganropa, Julie Ann is the English dub voice of Tenko Chabashira, while Sora Tokui is the Japanese voice.

11. Kira Buckland

Kira Buckland was born on July 16, 1987, in Anchorage, Alaska. She is an American voice actress who has provided voices for English dubbed Japanese anime, cartoons, and video games since 2004.

Kira voices the English version of Kirumi Tojo's voice in Danganronpa v3. Kikuko Inoue is the Japanese voice.

12. Erica Mendez

Erica Mendez is a Mexican-American voice-over actress and writer. She was born on February 13, 1988, in Chicago, Illinois. Mendez is known for her work on Erased (2016), Puyo Puyo Tetris (2014), and Hunter x Hunter (2011).

In the Japanese video game, Erica plays the English dub voice of Maki Harukawa. Maaya Sakamoto plays the Japanese voice.

13. Chris Tergliafera

Chris Tergliafera is an American voice actor and narrator who has established his career through animes and video games. One of his most prominent roles is Muhammad Avdol in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders.

Kyle Henry Hebert is an American national who works as a voice actor and DJ. He was born in Lake Charles, Louisiana, in June 1969 and is also referred to as Squeege. Kyle is famous for his work in anime and video game series.
