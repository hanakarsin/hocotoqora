Online passport application form in Tanzania 2020

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, May 31, 2024

A passport application form is one of the mandatory documents you need when you want a passport to travel abroad. Previously, every Tanzanian had to visit the immigration office or Tanzanian embassy close to them to get this important document. The process has become digital, and you can apply for your Tanzanian passport online without any hassle.

If you are a Tanzanian citizen and you need to travel abroad, you must get a Tanzanian passport. Over the last few years, the immigration office has introduced reforms in passport applications to keep up with the trends taking place in the online marketplace. The reforms have done away with the need for Tanzanians to visit the immigration offices when applying for passports. Instead, they can access this service online from the comfort of their home or office.

What is needed to acquire a passport?

If you are considering applying for a Tanzanian passport, there are specific documents you must provide when submitting your application to the immigration offices or online. These documents include:

  • The birth certificate of one of your parents, or in case the birth certificate is not available; you may provide an affidavit which confirms its existence

  • If you have not yet attained the age of 18 years, you must submit written consent from your guardian or parent. The consent grants minors permission to travel outside Tanzania.

Before starting the online application, ensure that all these documents are scanned because you will be required to upload them.

How do I get a passport application?

For you to get a passport, you are supposed to go to a nearby immigration Tanzania agency or Uhamiaji Tanzania office, where you will be offered a passport application form. If you do not live in Tanzania, you can access the service at the Tanzanian embassy. Remember to carry along all the necessary documents when going to the immigration office to submit your application to evade inconveniences. Here are the steps you should follow:

  • Go to the nearby immigration headquarters and ask for the passport application form.
  • Once you are handed the application form, fill it, and make sure to provide the right and accurate information.
  • Pay the application fee (the fee varies depending on the type of passport you are applying for)
  • Make sure you attach every necessary document and proceed to present your application form.
  • After submitting the application, the immigration officer will go through the documents and submit them for processing.
  • Your biometric data will be collected.
  • The immigration officer will issue a transcript showing the date when you will collect your passport. You will then go back to the same agency on the date indicated on your transcript to collect your passport.

Online passport application form Tanzania

Did you know you can access the Tanzanian passport application form online? The issuance of online passport application forms is a move by the immigration department to reduce congestion in its offices. So many applicants are forced to queue up to collect the forms, and this is inconveniencing to them. Immigration Tanzania online application can be completed in a few simple steps as follows:

  • Go to the online website of Tanzanian immigration and open it
  • Click on “e-service” on the menu bar. Scroll down the menu and then click on the “Passport Application Form.”
  • For a new passport application, tap the “New Application” tab
  • Feed the form with the prerequisite background details, including the type of application, your mobile number, place of residence, and the region where you will be traveling to.
  • Tap the “ Start Application” tab to begin your application procedure
  • Print out the reference application number indicated and save it for later
  • Fill the form with the accurate details
  • Make sure you attach all the essential documents
  • Go through the application and ensure the details you have provided are correct
  • Pay the application fee and retain a receipt
  • Submit the completed form. Check your email for a notification on the receipt of the application.

How long does it take to get a passport?

How long will you wait before collecting the passport from Uhamiaji offices? The waiting time varies depending on several factors. For example, if there are a lot of people who have applied for passports, it means the department is handling a high volume of applications. Hence, you need to exercise a little patience. It also depends on whether you are filing a new application, a replacement, a renewal, and so on.

It is important to note that if your application has no issues which need to be addressed, the waiting period is two weeks. If you apply for the passport online, use the passport tracking to confirm your application status and know when your passport is ready for collection.

Electronic passport in Tanzania

The E-passport is a traditional passport which has a microchip that carries a person’s identity information, Tanzanian citizens are advised to apply for e-passport since the current machine-readable passport expired on 31st January 2020. Here is a step by step procedure on how to apply e-passport Tanzania:

  • Visit the Tanzanian immigration web page and click on “start” to fill out the new application form
  • Click on “Continue” and fill the required information
  • Note down the application number somewhere as you will need it later
  • Provide information about your old passport
  • Attach the necessary documents together with the form
  • Make sure you review if the information is correct and click “agree.”
  • Make the payment fee of TZS 150,000 after receiving a control number. Ensure you make the payment on time before the expiry of the control number. Failure to do so will mean repeating the process from the start.
  • When you are done, download and print the application form and submit it to the nearby Uhamiaji Tanzania office
