SoLLUMINATI - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, July 25, 2024

YouTube gamer who posts NBA 2K content to his SoLLUMINATI channel. He has a secondary YouTube channel called SPIRITUAL So where he posts vlogs focused on profound subjects, with video titles like "LIFE after DEATH" and "truth about RELIGION." 

Before Fame

He played college basketball at Odessa College and Eastern Michigan University. He launched his YouTube channel in 2015.

Trivia

His SoLLUMINATI channel has more than 2 million subscribers, with over 236 million total views. His solluminati Instagram account has featured multiple videos of him dunking.

Family Life

His real name is Javontay Baynes. He hails from Detroit, Michigan. In 2018, he began dating OmgYoAsh, but the two broke up the following year. His younger brother Gawd Triller is also a YouTuber.

Associated With
