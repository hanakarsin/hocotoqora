Amina Chao: 10 interesting facts you need to know about Jalang'o's wife

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, June 30, 2024

It has been decades since people got to know comedian Felix Odiwuor. Yet, for the longest time, it never seemed like he was the kind of person eager to find a better half and make a life-long commitment until he met Amina Chao. To some people, he was just another playboy in town swimming in the fortunes begotten from his blossoming career as a comedian and media personality. However, the controversy under the hashtag Jalang'o's Boys Club revealed the mindset of netizens who believe that the comedian cannot sustain a marriage, but he continues to prove them wrong.

Little was known about Amina Chao before she hooked up with Jalang’o. Their union was officially stamped in a high profile ceremony that saw bigwigs in the entertainment industry attend. This thrust Amina into the public limelight, with fans eager to know all they can learn about her.

10 interesting facts about Amina Chao

Amina Chao and Jalang'o may not be ranked as the number one power couple in the country but are still role models to many. The two have had their ups and downs to the point where rumours claimed they had separated. Here are interesting facts about Jalang'o's wife:

1. She married Jalang’o in 2019

Chao and Jalang'o exchanged their vows on the 26th of October, 2019. The traditional wedding was graced by friends and family. The colourful ceremony was held in Syokimau on the outskirts of Nairobi.

Notable guests who attended include Jalang'o's friends Alex Mwakideu, Tom Japanni, MC Jessy, Chris Kirwa, and Big Ted. The couple has been married for close to two years now.

2. Amina Chao is a Muslim

Amina is one of the brave people not to let religion stand in her way to marry the person she love. Typically, she would have been expected to marry someone with whom they share the Muslim belief. However, that did not stop her from breaking the tradition and dating a man who does not share her faith and even married him.

In an interview, Jalang'o revealed that he is not a religious guy and hardly goes to church. He, however, can't resist the temptation to enter and pray when he passes by a church playing good music.

3. She is a Safaricom employee

Unlike most wives of celebrities who sit at home and rely on their husbands' money for upkeep, Amina has a promising career that can cater for her needs. By the time she got married, she was already an employee of telecommunication giant Safaricom. She falls under the General Trade Visibility and Events Department.

4. Amina is an alumnus of USIU

It is a tale of beauty with grains when it comes to Amina Chao. She attended the prestigious United States International University – Africa (USIU). She studied for a Bachelor of Arts Degree in International Relations and Affairs at the institution.

5. She gets along with Jalang’o’s ex, Cheptoek

It is almost unheard of to have an ex-girlfriend and the current girlfriend getting along. Usually, an ex is bitter that the current girlfriend stole her man, and the two tend not to see eye to eye. However, one thing that shows Amina is not a typical everyday girl is her ability to have a good relationship with Cheptoek.

Before he started seeing Amina, Jalang'o was in a relationship with Cheptoek Boyo, with whom they had two children. However, the relationship did not work out, and as a result, they went their separate ways. Cheptoek moved on with her life but has also maintained a good relationship with Jalang’o and Amina.

7. Amina and Cheptoek co-parent

Amina and Jalang'o's ex (Cheptoek) do not only get along well, but they also co-parent. This speaks to the character of Amina, who has no problem with being there for Jalang'o's children with his baby mama. The ex-girlfriend revealed that it has been fun interacting with Amina. This has been possible because they have prioritized the well-being of the children and put aside their issues.

8. She hails from the Coast

Amina comes from Mombasa County, which is home to the second-biggest city in the country. Her ethnicity is Taita revealing yet another beautiful thing about the family. Jalang'o is from Nyanza, but love brought the two people from different worlds together. While Amina is a Taita which is Bantu, Jalang'o is Nilote from the Luo tribe.

9. Amina named her first son Jamail

Jalang'o is quite the romantic man who would do anything for his queen. They named their son Jamail Edward, a Muslim name, to honour Amina being a Muslim. Naming children is an excellent deal because both parents usually have different ideas and want the names of their kin to be given to their children.

To balance the equation, their second child will probably have a Christian name. As for Jamail, names may not have too much significance at his age. That is why he prefers calling himself King Otuchi. Otuchi is the name of his grandfather.
