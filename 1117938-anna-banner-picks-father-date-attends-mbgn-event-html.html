Anna Banner picks her father as date as she attends MBGN event

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, April 22, 2024

It is generally believed that a lady should be accompanied by a guy when attending an event and that is exactly what 2013 Most Beautiful Girls in Nigeria (MBGN) in Nigeria Anna Banner did when she allowed her father to accompany to an event in Lagos recently.

Friday, July 28 was a day of reunion for the past MBGN as they met to herald the 2017 edition of the pageant which also doubles as the 30th edition of the pageantry.

One of the cynosures night was Anna Banner, 2013 MBGN queen. Four years after relinquishing the crown, Anna looks so beautiful you won’t believe she has a daughter already. The ex-beauty queen has her handsome father as her chaperone to the event and you realized she got her beauty from him.

Speaking at the event on how she won the crown Anna said she became the MBGN 2013 at the age of 18 without the help of anybody but God.

The 2017 search for a new MBGN commences with screening exercise holding August 8-12 while the grand finale is expected to hold on September 15 according to the vice president of Silverbird Group, Guy Murray Bruce.

Other ex-queen at the event includes reigning MBGN Unoaku ANyadike who spoke on the trending gender based violence. Former Mr Nigeria Bryan Okpara and Emmanuel Ikubese, MBGN 2006 Abiola Bashorun, Toke Makinwa and others.
