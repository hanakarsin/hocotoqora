Raila Odinga wows Kenyans with romantic birthday message to his lovely wife Mama Ida

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Opposition leader Raila Odinga and his wife have always been icons and a proper example of how a successful marriage should look like.

The two lovebirds are also never afraid of flaunting their romance and sending each other cute messages.

In a sweet message shared on Facebook and Twitter, the ODM leader celebrated his bae’s birthday in style.

A few eyes were left with balancing tears as netizens read the sweet birthday message Raila dedicated to his number one lady.

An excited Raila described his better half as a pillar of strength and guidance for him and their family throughout the years.

“Thank you for being a pillar of strength and guidance for us as a family all throughout the years. May God bless you on your special day. Happy Birthday Mama Ida Odinga,” he wrote.

Their children followed suit with the most adorable and loving messages and wished Ida nothing but happiness during her birthday.

“For you, I would trade the world. Happy birthday mama,” Winnie Odinga said.

Their son Raila Juniour was not left behind and also chimed in on the niceties.

“Whatever makes you feel happy and loved Mama, that is what I wish for you today. Happy birthday mom,” he said.
