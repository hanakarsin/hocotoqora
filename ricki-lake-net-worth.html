TV personality, actress, and producer Ricki Lake has made a significant mark in the entertainment industry with her dynamic career spanning several decades. Best known for her talk show, The Ricki Lake Show, which premiered in 1993 and quickly became one of the most talked-about daytime TV programs, Ricki has also made notable contributions to film and television.

Her breakout role in John Waters' 1988 film Hairspray as Tracy Turnblad brought her into the limelight, showcasing her talent to a wide audience. Beyond her on-screen presence, Ricki has ventured into production work, further diversifying her career and income sources. But, how much is Ricki Lake's net worth?

What is Ricki Lake's net worth?

Ricki Lake's recent 30-pound weight loss.

Recently, Ricki has opened up about her 30-pound weight loss. According to The Express, Ricki weighed 260 pounds while she was filming Hairspray.

Ricki shared on Instagram, "My husband, Ross joined me in this effort. Together we have each lost 30+ lbs. I really want to share, because we did this without relying on a pharmaceutical. (Not that there is anything wrong with that.) But neither of us were pre-diabetic and both of us felt like we wanted to at least try and do it on our own. Being 55 and in perimenopause, I was a bit worried, that my body would not drop the lbs like it had in the past. I am so so proud of us."

Her battle with her weight isn't the only thing Ricki has been open about, either. In 2017, Ricki lost her second husband, Christian Evans, to suicide two years after they divorced, per People. She shared, "It is with a heavy heart that I share that my beloved soulmate, Christian Evans has passed," she announced in an Instagram post at the time. "The world didn't understand this man, but I did. He succumbed to his lifelong struggle with bipolar disorder."
