Warri-Born Pere Egbi who has a net worth estimated at 20,000 dollars is a Nigerian and one of the most successful Big Brother Naija season 6 housemates, Nurse, actor and model who came into the spotlight following his appearance at the reality show in 2021.

Early Life/Educational Background

Born in Warri, Delta state, Pere was raised by a single mother and is the firstborn of two children. Pere grew up in Delta where he completed his primary and secondary school and obtained his first school leaving and his West African senior school certificate respectively. After his secondary school education, Pere proceeded to the university where he graduates with a degree in nursing and today he’s a professional nurse.

Personality and Character

The Warri-born believes changes is the only constant in Life and describes himself as a spontaneous, adventurous person. He is attracted to things of high value and is motivated by his past. As an empath, he hopes to use his experiences with pain to help heal others. He enjoys dancing, playing games, writing, bar_hopping, and water rafting.

Career

The nature born drama king is a qualified nurse and has served in the United States Military for six years becoming coming back home to pursue a career in real estate. He’s also a part-time actor and model.
