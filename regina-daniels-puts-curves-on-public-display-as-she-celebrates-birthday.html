Regina Daniels Puts Curves On Public Display As She Celebrates Birthday

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Nollywood actress and billionaire’s wife, Regina Daniels, has taken to social media to flaunt her curves as she celebrates her birthday today.

Naija News reports that the mother of two left many fans gushing over her beauty with the photos shared on her Instagram page.

In the photos, Regina Daniels was captured rocking a beautiful outfit, and lovely makeup while posing for the camera.

She captioned the photos: “Happy birthday to me. 10/10″

Rita Daniels Prays For Daughter

Regina’s mother, Rita also took to her Instagram page to shower prayers and birthday wishes on her daughter.

She noted that words cannot express how she feels, adding that Regina will continue to shine forth.

She wrote: “D day is bright and beautiful. I can hear d sounds of the birds singing. I can feel the dews drizzling. I can feel a gentle touch blessing the world. I can hear a still voice saying a queen is born today.

“When i woke up in my deepest sleep i saw all the dreams come true. It is a day single out for signs and wonders only because an apple of God’s eyes is sent to the world.

“To conquer.

“To save.

“To deliver

“And to set the captives free.

“Thank God i am the mother of these blessings. Words can’t express how i feel today. All i have to say is thank you JESUS.

“HAPPY BIRTHDAY MY SWEETNESS……SHINE FORTH DAUGHTER OF ZION @regina.daniels.”
