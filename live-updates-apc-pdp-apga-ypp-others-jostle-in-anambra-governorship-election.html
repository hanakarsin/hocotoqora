APC, PDP, APGA, YPP, Others Jostle In Anambra Governorship Election

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Residents of Anambra State are expected to come out in their numbers today to vote in the next governor to pilot the affairs of the state for the next four years.

Naija News reports that the major parties jostling for the state’s number one seat include the ruling All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA), All Progressives Congress (APC), Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) and Young Progressives Party (YPP).

The run-up to the election which has dominated media space like no other promises to be an intriguing affair.

Who amongst Senator Andy Uba, Valentine Ozigbo, Charles Soludo and Senator Ifeanyi Ubah would carry the day?

Stick with NaijaNews as we bring you first-time update on the Anambra governorship election…
