May will be a tough month for Churchill Show comedian and Hot 96 presenter Herman Gakobo Kago, aka Prof Hamo.

On Tuesday, May 11, DNA results confirmed that he was indeed the father of comedienne Jemutai's two children.

Jemutai accused Hamo of neglecting their kids, and things turned ugly as they aired each other's dirty linen in public, forcing Godfather of Kenyan comedy Churchill to intervene.

Well, TUKO.co.ke has managed to get photos of the two before their love turned sour, and below are five photos of them during their happy moments.

"I bought school uniforms, shoes, and stationaries for her two kids just like a good mum," she said.
