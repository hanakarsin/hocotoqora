Megan Weller - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Digital media influencer most notable for her video contributions on YouTube and TikTok. She publishes DIY content to the former network, including her most popular video "DIY POPSOCKET!!! Perfect for musical.ly!"

Before Fame

She first registered for YouTube in November of 2013. She posted her first video called "DIY Stitch Costume" in October of 2014.

Trivia

On TikTok, she posts a mix of comedy, challenge, lip-sync and DIY videos.

Family Life

She is from Pennsylvania.

Associated With

She has posted a couple of DIY bow videos inspired by the bows worn by JoJo Siwa.
