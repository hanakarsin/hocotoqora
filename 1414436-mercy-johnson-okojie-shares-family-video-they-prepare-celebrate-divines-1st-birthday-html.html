Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, February 26, 2024

- Mercy Johnson-Okojie and her family are gearing up to celebrate her fourth child, Divine's first birthday

- The Nollywood star took to her Instagram page to share a video of the birthday decoration set up by the family

- The actress and her husband who also shared a cute family photo, welcomed baby Divine on May 4, 2020
