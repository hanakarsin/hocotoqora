Nardwuar was born on July 5, 1968, in Vancouver, Canada. At birth, his parents gave him the name John Ruskin. The name of his father is Vernon Ruskin, while that of his mother is Olga Ruskin. His father worked as an engineer while his mother was a local journalist.

For his high school education, he attended the Hillside Secondary School in West Vancouver. During high school, he was one of the members of the school council. After graduating from high school, he enrolled at The University of British Columbia, where he graduated with a Bachelor's degree in history in 1990.

Career

He debuted his media career in October 1987 when he became the host of the weekly afternoon show on the University of Columbia radio station. The show used to run every Friday afternoon, and it featured music mixes, interviews, and commentary. He started interviewing celebrities and attracted media attention for his interviewing style.

Why is Nardwuar called the Human Serviette?
