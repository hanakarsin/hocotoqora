Ellen White - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Forward known for playing with Manchester City in the Women's Super League and on the English national women's team. She has also represented England at the international level and played with Great Britain for the 2016 and 2020 Summer Olympics. She has played on over 5 teams throughout her senior career. 

Before Fame

She got her start playing in the youth league with Arsenal from 1997 to 2005. She debuted at the senior level with Chelsea in 2005. 

Trivia

In February 2021, she broke the record for most goals scored in the Women's Soccer League with 55 goals. She was named England's best player of the year in 2011, 2018, and 2021.

Family Life

She is married to a man named Callum Convery. She comes from a family of West Ham United F.C. fans.

Associated With

She beat the record for the most goals scored in the WSL by Dutch star Vivianne Miedema, who had 54 goals.
