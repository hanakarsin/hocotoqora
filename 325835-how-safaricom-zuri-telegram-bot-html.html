How to use Safaricom Zuri telegram bot

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Tired of waiting in line, physically or on a call, when you have an issue with your carrier's services? With a vast clientele all over the country, Safaricom, Kenya’s leading mobile carrier, is synonymous with long waiting lines. Therefore, intending to reduce the customer frustration that comes with dialling 100, the firm decided to enhance its customer service delivery via the introduction of chatbot technology. Named Zuri – the chatbot represents a new era in customer care, where brands choose to actively engage their clients via social media and other possible platforms. Wondering how to use Safaricom Zuri Telegram bot?

Sure, you’ve interacted with bots on other platforms, knowingly or unknowingly, as they are all over the internet. It is the capability of handling simple and repetitive tasks that makes bot technology a god-sent. The Zuri app can handle various queries faster than humans would. It is available on two popular and secure messaging services – Telegram and Facebook messenger. The former is the preferred avenue for many, considering the software’s strict adherence to privacy. Why not take advantage of artificial intelligence via the Safaricom chatbot and get all your problems sorted out?

Reversing M-Pesa transactions using Safaricom chatbot

How to get M-Pesa Till statement

Are you interested in knowing how to use Safaricom Zuri Telegram bot for those erroneous M-Pesa transactions? There’s no need to start panicking and causing more blunders while trying to dial the customer care number or to send a reversal SMS. All you need to do is to access the bot on Telegram.

When opened, you’ll get the usual pleasantries and options to choose from. Go to the “M-Pesa customer to customer reversal” section in the menu options. The bot will then respond by requesting you to type the transaction code or to paste the entire message on the platform. Voila! Wait for the reversal to be facilitated.

It is a feature that most people have been looking forward to one that is less cumbersome than other procedures.

How to reverse a PesaPal transaction

It is a common occurrence for many subscribers, and interestingly, Zuri makes the unsubscribing process quite easy. Immediately head to the “chargeable and promotional SMS” menu, input your phone number and tap on “unsubscribe all.” In minutes, your problem’s sorted.

How to use Safaricom Zuri Telegram bot for airtime top-up

Well, the available methods are pretty straightforward. You can utilize either a scratch card (who uses this any longer with the various technological advances) or the other obvious strategy – M-Pesa.

So, in case you still utilize scratch cards and have yours ready, you can complete the rest of the process using the Zuri app on Telegram. Assuming that you are already on the main menu, click on the "products and services," then head to "airtime top-up" and choose "scratch card."

The bot will respond by asking for the phone number to be topped up and then request the voucher PIN. Enter the 16 digit number, and the airtime goes directly to your number or someone else's if you were topping up another number. Easy, right?

Getting a prepaid usage statement using Safaricom Zuri services

Wondering about your prepaid account airtime usage? In the past, getting this statement involved a lot of bureaucracies; however, with the newly introduced (nearly a year old) bot feature on Telegram, getting a statement is easy.

Once you are on the Safaricom chatbot’s interface, you’ll spot the option of “prepaid usage statement” in the menu. When you click it, you’ll be directed to a screen where you can choose the period of the statement, which can either be for the last three to ten months.

After choosing either option, the bot will ask you to write your number, followed by your email address. As an extra measure to keep your account safe, you’ll get a prompt to enter your M-Pesa pin before the final submission. Once successfully validated, the statement instantly arrives in your mailbox.
