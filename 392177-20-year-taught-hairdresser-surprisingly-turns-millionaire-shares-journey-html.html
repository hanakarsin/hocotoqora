20-year-old self-taught hairdresser incredibly becomes a millionaire

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, July 29, 2024

- Nia The Loc God, a beautiful young lady on Twitter has become a self-made millionaire after starting out as a hairdresser with no formal training

- The young lady simply began making hair for herself and her mom at home when she was 17 and eventually got recruited in a hair-braiding firm

- Along the line, the pretty girl decided to start her product line and it has become exceedingly successful to her amazement

A beautiful black girl identified on Twitter as Nia The Loc God and the handle, @Niathelocgod, has revealed she is now a millionaire to the surprise of her followers.

In a post seen by TUKO.co.ke on her personal Twitter handle, the young lady indicated that it all started when she was 17 and decided to begin making hair without any formal training.

According to the brilliant 20-year-old millionaire, she simply got off doing her own hair and that of her mother at home.

Check out her post below:

From that point on, Nia The Loc God was found as a skillful braider by a local braiding firm in her country and recruited to start working.

The young lady gained a lot of experience braiding for hundreds of customers and decided to take her career a step further by starting a product line when she was 19.

After barely a year when the product line began, Nia The Loc God is now a full-time CEO whose net worth has been estimated to be over a million US Dollars.

This is what Nia The Loc God had to say about her achievements.
