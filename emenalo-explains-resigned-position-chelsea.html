Emenalo explains why he resigned his position at Chelsea

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

-Emenalo describes his time at Chelsea as successful and enjoyable.

-The former Super Eagles defender also explains family reasons were behind his decision to resign.

Emenalo talks about his time at Chelsea FC.

Former Super Eagles defender, Michael Emenalo has cited family reasons for his sudden decision to resign his position as technical director of English Premier league side, Chelsea.

Emenalo stated, “After 10 years here, 10 wonderful, successful years, but very demanding years, it is a very tough decision to decide to step aside.

“It is entirely my decision and it has come about for very simple reasons. I need an opportunity to get to see my young kids grow and also to step back and reflect on the work that I have done here and the things that we have been able to accomplish together in this great club.

“It is something I have been thinking about for quite some time now and it is something I have discussed with my family and they understand the reasons and the timing for wanting to step aside.”

The former Super Eagles star also spoke on the loan system he managed at the Stamford Bridge, addressing why several young talents have to go out on loan before returning to the club, making particular reference to Andreas Christensen.

“He’s shown great humility to go on loan and work very hard while he was there and gain the experience which is needed, and the timing is right for him now to step in and show what he’s learnt during those periods of development.”
