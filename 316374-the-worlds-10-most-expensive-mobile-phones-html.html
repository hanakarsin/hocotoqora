The World's 10 Most Expensive Mobile Phones

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, May 17, 2024

Everyone loves luxury items! Here is a list of the top 10 most expensive phones in the world which are made for the royals and high class. Note: rating was published on November 4, 2014.

1. IPhone 5 Black Diamond. Price Tag – $15.3 Million

One of the richest brands in the world made a phone coated with 26 Carat of black diamond.

2. IPhone 4 Diamond Rose Edition. Price Tag – $8 Million

The 2nd most expensive mobile is the IPhone 4 which is coated with multiple diamond rose edition.

3. IPhone 3GS Supreme Goldstriker Advanced. Price Tag – $3.2 millions

IPhone 3GS is one more device for luxury people or the millionaires.

4. IPhone 3G Kings Button. Price Tag – $2.4 million

One more luxury mobile is the IPhone 3G Kings Button which has a button made of the most expensive carat diamond of 6.6 edition.

5. GoldVish Le Million. Price Tag – $1.3 million

GoldVish Le Million is covered with only gold and is made for people who love the Luxury edition.

6. Diamond Crypto Smart Phone. Price Tag – $1.3 million

The best thing in Diamond Crypto Smart Phone is that it has been totally covered with diamonds.

7. Gresso Luxor Las Vegas Jackpot. Price Tag – $1 million

Gresso Luxor Las Vegas Jackpot is a masterpiece of design.

8. Vertu Signature Cobra. Price Tag – $310,000

9. Sony Ericsson Black Diamond. Price Tag – $310,000

10. IPhone Princess Plus. Price Tag – $176,400

So, what is your dream phone?
