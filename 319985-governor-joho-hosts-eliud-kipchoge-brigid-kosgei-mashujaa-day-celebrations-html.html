Governor Joho hosts Eliud Kipchoge, Brigid Kosgei ahead of Mashujaa Day celebrations

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, March 8, 2024

Mombasa Governor Hassan Joho on Saturday, October 19, hosted over 200 heroes and heroines ahead of Mashujaa Day celebration set to be held at Mama Ngina Waterfront Park on Sunday, October 20.

Among those hosted by the county boss included marathoners Eliud Kipchoge and Brigid Kosgei.
