Jigawa State, Nigeria

Jigawa State is located in the northwestern region of Nigeria. It was created on August 27, 1991, when it was separated from Kano State. The capital city of Jigawa State is Dutse.

Population

According to the 2006 census, the population of Jigawa State was 4,361,002. As of 2021, the estimated population of Jigawa State is 4,000,000.
