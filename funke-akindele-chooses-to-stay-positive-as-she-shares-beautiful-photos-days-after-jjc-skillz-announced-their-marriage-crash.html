Funke Akindele Chooses To Stay Positive Days After JJC Skillz Announced Their Marriage Crash

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, March 15, 2024

Nollywood actress and content creator Funke Akindele has chosen to stay positive and maintain focus amidst her marriage crash with music producer Abdulrasheed Bello popularly called JJC Skillz.

Recall that on 30th June 2022, JJC Skillz took to his Instagram page to announce the end of their 7 years old marriage.

He said the last two years of their marriage have extremely been difficult and had moved out of the house three months ago on Funke’s insistence.

Since then, Funke Akindele has remained silent on the issue instead of engaging in social media drama with JJC.

Stepping out for the first time since the news of her marriage crashed hit the internet, Funke took to her Instagram page to share a video of herself rocking an Ankara outfit.

The actress who seemed excited also showed off her dancing skills as she gives different poses for the camera.

She captioned the video with several hashtags such as “stay positive, stay brave, stay ambitious, stay focused and stay strong.”
