Tammy Slaton - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, March 22, 2024

Reality television personality who rose to fame after appearing on TLC's weight-loss series 1000-LB Sisters'. At the start of the program she weighed more than 600 pounds.

Before Fame

She could not walk without the help of a walker.

Trivia

She was featured in a 2019 article published by People magazine. 

Family Life

She appeared on 1000-LB Sisters alongside her sister Amy Slaton. She has a brother named Chris Combs who also appears on the show. 

Associated With

Both she and Kate Gosselin appeared on TLC programs.
