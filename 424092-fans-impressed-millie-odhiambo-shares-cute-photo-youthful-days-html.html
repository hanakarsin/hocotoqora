Fans Impressed After Millie Odhiambo Shared Cute Photo of Her Youthful Days

  • It is no doubt that Suba North MP Millie Odhiambo has gained a large fan base thanks to her bravery and outspokenness
  • The vocal member of parliament recently shared on Facebook a cute throwback photo of her youthful
  • Many Kenyans were impressed by how she has maintained her code of dressing throughout the ages

It is no doubt that the outspoken Suba North MP Millie Odhiambo has gained a large fan base thanks to her fearless personality.

The 51-year-old advocate shared a beautiful throwback photo of herself and her friend on Facebook.

It is indicated in the footnote of the photo that it was snapped in 2009 when Millie was younger and more energetic.

Comparatively, Millie's face and body appearance has not changed much.

Millie stepped into parliament as a nominated MP in 2008 thanks to the ODM party.

She later won in the consequent general election and has been an elected MP to date.

Many Kenyans on social media were impressed by the beautiful TBT photo and most of them camped at the comment section with praises.

Millie Odhiambo celebrates husband

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, Millie recently took to her social media account to celebrate her Zimbabwean hubby, Magugu Mabona on his birthday.

"It's been a year of COVID-19 but with blessings of the love of family. Happy birthday prince charming," she captioned photos of them posted on Facebook on Thursday, June 3.

The vocal MP also shared photos of them having memorable moments with their children.
