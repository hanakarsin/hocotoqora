How TripleMG Fell Apart After Losing Two A-list Artistes

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Music executive and talent manager, Ubi Franklin has taken to his social media to reveal how his brand, TripleMG shattered after losing its two A-list artistes, Tekno and Iyanya.

Naija News reports that the father of four recounted how he lost his confidence after everything he built over the years fell apart in 2018.

While urging fans to trust the process and God, Ubi maintained, he got back his top singers who had earlier left, adding that he had over the years rebuilt the brand to standard.

He further stressed that his brand now has over 30 HIT records and a total 230 songs are under his label management.

“For people who do not know, I have more than 30 HIT records under my record label, @mademenmusicmg from @iyanya x @teknomiles. Everything was going fine and in 2016 lost Iyanya left and Tekno stayed back till 2018 December and I had to build back again, I lost confidence, I thought I couldn’t do it again, but I pushed forward, today I have them back and more Big Artiste I manage. From 2009 till date I have released more than 230 songs under my company. We keep pushing, more to come.

“Made money and I have lost a lot of money, I just wana write a book, been through good and bad times and God keep us going. Only God can keep you going, Always trust God”.
