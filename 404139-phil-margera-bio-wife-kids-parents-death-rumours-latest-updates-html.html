Phil Margera biography

Phil’s siblings include the late Vincent, Belinda O’Donnell, Patrick, Kevin, Ruth Clark, and Darlene Orange.

He is 5 feet 10 inches (1.8 m) tall and weighs 142 kilograms. Phil Margera’s eye colour is blue, and he has brown hair.
