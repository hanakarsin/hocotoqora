Who's on the Roster for 'MultiVersus'? It Leaked Online

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

The upcoming roster for 'MultiVersus' has leaked online. Here are the fighters who will soon appear in the popular Warner Bros. fighting game.

These days, resourceful people on the internet can find leaks for just about anything. Fans discovered the multiverse-shattering cameos for Spider-Man: No Way Home months before the movie was released in theaters. Capcom had to address a massive leak to their upcoming title, Street Fighter 6. Then there's MultiVersus, the popular fighting game that pits Warner Bros. characters like Bugs Bunny and Batman against each other in Super Smash Bros.-like brawls.

Weeks before the game officially went live, fans had already uncovered leaks detailing several playable characters who would be added to the title's already eclectic roster. Continue reading to see if your most-wanted character makes it into MultiVersus.

Here are all the characters on the leaked 'MultiVersus' roster so far.

When MultiVersus was first announced in November 2021, the game already featured quite the unexpected lineup of fighters. The platform fighter draws from several familiar Warner Bros. properties like DC Comics and Looney Tunes, but even the world-renowned Game of Thrones has representation in the game. As of this writing, players can choose to play as Batman, Bugs Bunny, Steven Universe, and even Arya Stark among others!

'MultiVersus' Season 2 may feature a couple of surprises.

Season 1 of MultiVersus added plenty of new characters who were part of the initial leak. Rick, Morty, Gizmo, Stripe, and Black Adam all made it to the game throughout the game's first few months of live services. With Season 2 officially underway, fans may have already discovered and datamined who will be added to the cast next!

Article continues below advertisement

As an official announcement, the dev team at Player First Games confirmed in a blog post that Looney Tunes' Marvin the Martian is coming to the game. The team has confirmed that he is simply "one of the new characters" coming to the cast, with more expected to follow. The game will also add a Game of Thrones-themed stage set in the Iron Throne hall as well as new Battle Pass rewards and costumes.

Aside from official confirmations, fans have already datamined Season 2 and may have discovered who's coming next.

Consistent with the aforementioned leaks, Samurai Jack might be added to the MultiVersus roster in the new update. But in a surprise twist that the original leaks didn't catch right away, we might also see characters from the 1985 action-adventure film, The Goonies.

MultiVersus is currently available on PlayStation, Xbox, and PC.
