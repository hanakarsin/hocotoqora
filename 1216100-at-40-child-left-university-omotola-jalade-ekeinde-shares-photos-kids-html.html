At 40 my last child has left for university - Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde says as she shares photos of ki

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

In recent times, Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde has been counting her blessings and showing just how much blessings and favour she has received last year and even this new season. Recently, the lady took to sharing a very good news with her fans.

This news has everything to do with Michael Ekeinde, Omotola's last child. The actress revealed that he gained admission into a reputable school.

In excitement, the beautiful actress took to her Instagram page to share photos of herself with her kids. In the very first picture, she was hugging her son in a show of celebration. The excited new student also hugged her back and grinned largely.

Other pictures of Omotola rejoicing and being carefree with her kids were also shared. The screen goddess was especially glad that her last kid was leaving for university at the exact time she became 40.
