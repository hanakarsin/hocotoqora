Nigerians Mock Bashir Ahmad As He Loses House Of Reps Ticket

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, February 9, 2024

Netizens have taken to social media to mock President Muhammadu Buhari’s social media aide, Bashir Ahmad, after he lost his House of Representatives ticket on the platform of the All Progressives Congress (APC).

Naija News reports that the 30-year-old indigene of Kano State contested for the Albasu/Gaya/Ajingi Federal Constituency seat which is currently being occupied by a member of the ruling party, Abdullahi Mahmud Gaya but lost.

Speaking of his experience at the primaries venue, Bashir disclosed that he had to leave the venue of the elections due to the numerous thugs present.

He further added that thugs shouldn’t be part of any elections.

He wrote; “As an aspirant, i left the venue of primary elections for Gaya, Ajingi and Albasu Federal Constituency, because of the security of our majority delegates, if want to compete with the best, thugs shouldn’t be part of any election. A dekace mu!”

Reacting to the reports of his loss, netizens mocked the presidential aide over his inability to secure his party ticket despite resigning from his role.
